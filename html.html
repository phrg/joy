Social Change Project: Joy and Happiness

Hello, and welcome to my social change project. My topic is joy. What I wanted to do through the things I wrote and created was to spread happiness and joy throughout the world. There were many ways I did this. I made this website, for example, using new skills I learned from my fellow classmate. I also made a scratch project, which will be linked below. I read articles on joy and happiness and wrote my findings, I watched speeches and other videos on joy, and lots of other things to figure out one question, how can joy and happiness change the world?

What is Joy?

What is joy, you may ask. Joy is different than happiness. Joy is an inner feeling, rather than an outward expression. Joy is a choice, while happiness is not. Finding joy could be as simple as going on a walk, or as big as getting married, for example. Certain objects bring joy to some people as well. Bubbles, rubber ducks, rainbows, etc. All these things have the same effect on our brain.
